More about RFISR


The Roaring Fork Invasive Species Reporting app is my way of giving back to the environment I grew up in. There are three sections of the app. The first section is where you can go to report invasive species to Colorado Parks and Wildlife. The second section is a list of invasive species. The third section is about me and why I created the app. This app was created using React Native.

The app is currently in the beta stage and is not available for download. Below is a video of the app in action.
