More about me!


I grew up surrounded by technology and that eventually turned into my passion. I came to college wanting to do programming and chose Advancing Computer Science as my major. After learning more about the classes the University of Advancing Technology offered and doing research, a dual major in Artificial Intelligence felt like the right move for me. I am proud of what I have learned and am excited to develop my skillset further.

Some of my hobbies besides programming are skiing, hiking, photography, listening to music, and playing video games. I also love to learn new things so you'll see me reading books or watching educational videos.

I am currently a Student Ambassador for the University of Advancing Technology. While I enjoy the Student Ambassador role, I am also looking for any intership opporunities in the tech field.
